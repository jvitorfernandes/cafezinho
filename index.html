Cafezinho

It is a Portuguese word that means "a little coffee", but is often used in Brazil to have a much deeper meaning. It is a word that simultaneously means "welcome".

"Ca-fe-zeen-yo"

In Brazil, it's used by businessmen to signal that it's time to take a break in the negotiations. It's during this time that the two sides put away the work and get to know each other on a more personal level. They talk about what is truly important: FAMILY! So, please come in, relax, get to know OUR family, and welcome!
